War

Card Game AI Challenge

I designed a captivating single-player card game application that emulates the classic 'War' card game. In this digital adaptation, players face off against an AI opponent, taking turns flipping cards onto the battlefield. The player with the higher-ranked card claims both, and in the case of a tie, a 'War' scenario unfolds, adding extra cards to the stakes. The game concludes when the player either accumulates all 52 cards or depletes their deck. This interactive and strategic card game offers an engaging experience for those seeking a challenging match against an AI opponent.
